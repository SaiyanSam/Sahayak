About Sahayak

Sahayak is a platform created to help the IIT Bombay community start and support fundraisers for various causes. Whether it's for personal needs, community projects, or charitable donations, our platform provides an easy and efficient way to raise funds.

Our mission is to empower the IIT Bombay community by providing them with the tools they need to achieve their financial goals and make a meaningful impact in the lives of people associated with IIT Bombay. At Sahayak, we believe that small contributions can make a big difference, and together, we can create positive change.

Once the platform is fully operational, it can also be expanded to connect with other organizations sharing similar values, leading to a broader impact.

If you have any questions or want to learn more about our platform, feel free to contact us.
